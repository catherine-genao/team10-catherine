Curly

Proteins such as keratins tend to build up unevenly in curly hair, collecting in the curve of your hair, make it curl even more.

Coily/kinky

“Kinky” describes the curl pattern/type of hair, like curly or wavy and in this case, kinky hair means tightly curled hair or really tight coiled hair or hair with no curls but zigzags instead. “Coarse” or “fine” describes the thickness/texture of hair strands.

Straight

People with straight hair have symmetrical follicles that grow round hair rather than oval hair.

wavy

the texture of hair also is dependent on the shape of the hair shaft. Follicles that are round in cross-section give rise to straight hair. Those out of which curly hair grows are oval. .
